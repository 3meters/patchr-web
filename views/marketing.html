Proximity computing.

Your phone does a great job helping you find where to go. But once you arrive, how does it help? Often its chirps only draw your attention away.

What if you could tune your phone down to room-scale, to the people, goings on, nobs, rules, schedule, traditions, of the space where you stand right now? That is proximity computing.

Patchr

Patchr is a Seattle-based startup exploring room-scale mobile apps, apps you launch when you arrive.
