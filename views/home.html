Patchr is life engaged, on and offline. We value making the most of your time in relation to your authentic experiences. In order to build a tool that felt integrated to real-time experiences, Patchr started with private patches that are connected to location. Public bulletin boards offer a foundation for discovery through open sharing and valuable information. But we feel private and secret patches are the key to designing a space where users are free to be candid, and to collaborate in a closed forum. Messages are stored in a physical context, where this interaction matters most.
